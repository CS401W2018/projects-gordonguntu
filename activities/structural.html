Structural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)

1. What goes in the header?

The title,logo of the website and possible navigation links goes in the header.

2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Some links that lead to the different sections or parts of the webpage or to different pages in the same website, it doesn't contain links to other websites. it is usually inside the header but sometimes it can be above or below the header or in a sidebar.

3. What goes in Main?

The main content of the website and all the informatio the website needs will be in the Main.

4. What goes in the Footer?

The coppyright claim, contact information and other resources that will be helpful are in the Footer.
