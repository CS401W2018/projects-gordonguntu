New Delhi - The Capital of India

link link link

New Delhi is the capital city of India. It is known for it's great historic significance, holds the culture of India, as well as the location of India's national government. It has many buildings that were built during ancient time. Delhi has two parts old Delhi ( built by the mughals) and new Delhi.

The picture to the left is called India Gate. It stands in the centre of Delhi for about 42m high. It signifies the 70,000 soldiers who fought like warriors and died for the country during world war 1. It exemplifies unity, bravery and courage that Indian Army soldiers displayed during the war.
